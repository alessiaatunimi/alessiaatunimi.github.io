Phd Student at Connets Lab, where I am also the manager of the website. My research activity focuses on the evolution and co-evolution of networks. During my PhD, I would like to leverage graph evolution rules and change point detection to develop theoretical models that analyze how networks evolve. Then, the models will be applied to large-scale graphs of different nature, like social or economic real-world networks. The final aim is to develop a multi-layer co-evolution model that can explain evolutionary mechanisms with the aid of external and internal events.
